Khachapuri

Description

This is a traditional dish from georgia, made with special georgian cheese(Sulguni).
But you can also use feta with mozzarella, it's delicious.Try it out!

Here are the ingredients you need:

  • Butter 250gr
  • Yoghurt 500ml
  • Feta 250gr
  • Mozzarella 250gr
  • Baking Powder 1 Tsp
  • Salt 1 Tsp
  • Dough appr. 750gr
  • 1 Egg

Preparation Steps:

  1. Melt the butter on low temperature.
  2. In a big Bowl fill in the Yoghurt, Baking Powder and add a bit of salt.
  3. Add the melted butter to the Yoghurt.
  4. Add the dough to the rest and mix everything with your hands.
  5. Create a non sticky dough and let it rest for 30min.
  6. In another Bowl put Feta with Mozzarella, add an Egg and mix everything together with your hands.
  7. Spread out the dough with a rolling pin into thin circular shapes, make a ball of cheese with your hands and put it in the middle of the dough.
  8. Close it down, so that the cheese is in between the dough and spread it out again with the rolling pin. Add flour to not make it stick.
  9. Put it on a heated pan until you see that the bottom side is ready.
  10. Then turn it over and do the same with the other side.
  11. Add butter to melt on both sides. It enhances the taste
  12. Et Voila, ready is your Khachapuri, Enjoy!
